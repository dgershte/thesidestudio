Useless Kiwi

A short lipsynching exercise for my 2D animation class at the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology. We had to come up with a character and a monologue for the character. Coming up with a monologue was much harder than I thought. And I'm still not sure why the Kiwi is russian.

What is EDI?

This was a second animation I created for Jonar Systems. It is a 5-minute short created in Flash to explain to the general user what EDI software is.

What is ERP?

The first short I created for Jonar Systems Inc to explain what ERP software is. I spent my first work term working for Jonar in Montreal developing and designing their website, and for the last few weeks I was asked to create this Flash animation. It was my first time animating anything so I was pretty excited about getting the opportunity to animate for a company. The video hit 100,000 views in just under a year, and eventually lead to creating a sequel - What is EDI?

Ostrich

This is a 20-second short where we were given a sound clip, and had to come up with an animation to match it. All the drawings were done by hand, over a lightbox, and then scanned into TVPaint, where the sound was added and exported to an Quicktime animation.
